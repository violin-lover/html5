How to Calculate Square Roots

A fun way to calculate square roots!

What is your guess?



Here is how we calculate the approximate value of a number's square root!

1. Make an educated guess. You could make a random guess too, but it would take a long time to figure it out.

2. Take the number you are trying to find the square root of, and divide it by your guess.

3. Add your quotient to your original guess.

4. Take the sum of the previous step and divide it by two. In other words, you halve it.

5. Now, set that as your new guess, and repeat the steps starting from step 2 again. The more you do it, the more accurate it will get.
